How iTabs Changed Content Forever

by David Chen | April 21, 2025

In the spring of 2024, I was struggling with a problem that every affiliate marketer knows too well: visitors clicking away to research products and never returning.

The Lightbulb Moment

The solution came to me during a conversation with my business partner. "What if we could keep visitors on our page while still providing them all the information they need?" That's when the concept of interactive popupsi was born.

Interactive popups display contextual information without leaving the page:
• Keep visitors engaged
• Provide detailed information instantly
• Prevent navigation away from your content

Key benefits:
• 40% higher conversion rates
• Reduced bounce rates
• Improved user experience
• Better information retention
